Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Cardinal collects, how it is used, and your choices as a user.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Messages and questions you submit through the Ask feature
  • Verses, highlights, and notes you create within the App

Automatically collected information:

  • A randomly generated, anonymous device identifier (not linked to your name or Apple ID)
  • App usage data (features used, session length) for improving the App
  • Subscription status (active or inactive) via Apple StoreKit — we do not receive your payment details

Information we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Your precise location
  • Contacts, photos, or other sensitive device data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and operate the App and its features
  • Process your questions through the AI Ask feature
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of the App
  • Enforce our Terms of Use and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI Feature and Third-Party Processing

When you use the Ask feature, your message and conversation history are sent to our backend server, which uses a third-party AI service to generate responses.

By using the Ask feature, you consent to this processing. Please do not include personally sensitive information (such as your full name, home address, or medical details) in Ask messages.

We take reasonable steps to ensure our third-party providers handle data securely and in accordance with applicable law, but we cannot guarantee the practices of third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Conversation data sent to the Ask feature is used to generate a response and is not permanently stored by the Developer in a way that identifies you.
  • Verses, highlights, and notes are stored locally on your device. Using Settings → Reset All Data deletes this information.
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple and retained per Apple's policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the App (e.g., our backend AI provider), under confidentiality obligations
  • As required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Developer or others

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at the address below and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access or delete information associated with your device identifier, the right to opt out of certain data processing, and rights under GDPR (EEA residents), CCPA (California residents), or other applicable laws.

To exercise these rights, contact us at Bridges@duck.com. Because we do not collect identifying information, we may need to work with you to verify your request.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
